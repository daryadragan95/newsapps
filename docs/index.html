Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Package name: com.newswinnerapp.app

This Privacy Policy explains how NewsWinner ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Android application.

Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The App may process the following information to provide its features:

  • Sports content, match data, team data, and news loaded from third-party sources.
  • Locally saved favorite teams stored on your device.
  • Notification preferences and scheduled match reminders.
  • WebView cookies, local storage, and website data when WebView content is opened.
  • Files or photos selected by you when a website inside WebView requests file upload.

Firebase Remote Configuration Gate

The App uses Firebase Firestore to read a remote configuration value from collection config, document app, field url. This value determines whether the App opens native screens or a WebView page.

If a WebView URL is received, it may be stored locally in SharedPreferences so that the App can continue opening the same WebView experience later, even if the remote configuration value becomes empty.

WebView, Cookies, and Third-Party Websites

When WebView is opened, the website displayed inside it may use cookies, JavaScript, DOM storage, and other web technologies. Those websites may collect information according to their own privacy policies. We do not control third-party websites and are not responsible for their privacy practices.

Camera and File Uploads

The App may request camera access only when a website inside WebView asks you to take or upload a photo, for example to photograph a document. Photos are provided to the website only after you choose to take or select them.

Downloads

If you download a file from WebView, the App may use the Android Download Manager to save the file to your device. Downloaded files are controlled by your device and Android system settings.

Notifications

The App may ask permission to send notifications. Notifications are used for match reminders, such as reminders before a match starts. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services and data providers, including Firebase, TheSportsDB, ESPN RSS feeds, and websites opened inside WebView. These services may process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores some settings locally on your device, such as favorite teams, cached WebView URL values, WebView website data, and cookies. You can remove this data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through a third-party website opened in WebView, please contact that website directly and review its privacy policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page with the updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app owner through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/daryadragan95/newsapps.
