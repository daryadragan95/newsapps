Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Package name: com.newswinnerapp.app

This Privacy Policy explains how News Sport Winner ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Android application.

Information We Collect

The App does not require users to create an account and does not ask for personal profile information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The App may process the following information to provide its features:

  • Sports content, match data, team data, and news loaded from third-party sources.
  • Locally saved favorite teams stored on your device.
  • Notification preferences and scheduled match reminders.

Firebase

The App may use Firebase services to support app configuration and reliability. Firebase may process technical information according to Google's privacy policy.

Notifications

The App may ask permission to send notifications. Notifications are used for match reminders, such as reminders before a match starts. You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services and data providers, including Firebase, TheSportsDB, and ESPN RSS feeds. These services may process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores some settings locally on your device, such as favorite teams, notification settings, and app preferences. You can remove this data by clearing the App's storage or uninstalling the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact the app owner.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page with the updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app owner through the GitHub repository: https://github.com/daryadragan95/newsapps.
